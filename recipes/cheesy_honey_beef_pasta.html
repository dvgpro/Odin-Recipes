Cheesy Honey Beef Pasta

Cheesy Honey Beef Pasta Recipe

This sweet, creamy pasta is a delicious meal that anyone can enjoy. High in carbs and protein, this filling dish is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 300g of shell pasta
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 2 tbsp of fajita seasoning
  • 3 tsp of garlic puree
  • 2 tbsp of tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp of honey
  • 300ml of beef stock
  • 120ml single cream, or heavy cream
  • 60g of mozzarella
  • Dash of salt and pepper
  • Optional: Parsley and chili flakes

How to Make

  1. Boil pasta in salt water, follow packaging cooking time
  2. Fry beef in large pan until in starts to brown
  3. Add garlic powder, paprika, and fajita seasoning, and mix
  4. Add diced onions, cook until soft
  5. Add honey, garlic puree, and tomato puree, cook for a few minutes while stirring
  6. Add stock and cream, simmer until sauce has thickened
  7. Add pasta, mozzarella, and remaining seasoning
  8. Mix until cheese has melted, then serve
