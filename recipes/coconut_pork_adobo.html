Filipino Coconut Pork Adobo

Filipino Coconut Pork Adobo Recipe

Enjoy the spicy, yet savory flavors of this traditional Filipino dish. Being the national dish of the Phillipines, you can experience the local delicasies in the comfort of your own home. With minimal prep time and some regional spice, you can expertly create this dish for you and your family to Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 500g of pork belly
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tsp of black peppercorns
  • 1 chicken cube
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of milk

How to Make

  1. Warm vegetable oil in large pan on medium-high heat
  2. Saute garlic and onions for 1 minute
  3. Add pork belly and sear on all side until brown
  4. Add vinegar, soy sauce, bay leave, black peppercorns, chicken cube, and water
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and let simmer until water is nearly gone
  7. Add brown sugar and coconut water
  8. Mix, and let it simmer until sauce has thickened
  9. Serve with hot, steamed rice
